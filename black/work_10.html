distance fog

Laurus Edelbacher

2022

distance fog: real time 3D animation from a virtual trash can. It absorbs and eliminates digital clutter - folders and images - from a constantly updating news feed.

The scene is set within a thick digital fog that enhances the depth and atmosphere. This ‘fogging’ is a technique from the 3D graphics called distance fog, to hide graphical imperfections and limitations.

The camera navigates this digital landscape in a continuous, looping motion. This smooth, deliberate movement allows viewers to experience the scene as an accelerating unstable digital system.

Data creation and deletion, and the struggle of systems to maintain coherence amid logical inconsistencies and conflicts.
