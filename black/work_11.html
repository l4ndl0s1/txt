cell.txt

Laurus Edelbacher

2014

Cell.txt — altering the syntax of HTML— the foundational language of the web — the artwork distorts the conventional presentation of digital content. This distortion is achieved by the removal of initial characters from HTML tags.

Leading the content to lose its familiar form and structure. This process uncovers the typically invisible language of the web environment.

It invites viewers to contemplate how digital language dictates form, and how slight alterations in this language can dramatically shift our perception. This shows the underlying syntax that shapes our online experiences.

How does the inherent language of a medium influence our interaction with it? What happens when the rules of this language are altered?
