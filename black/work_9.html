overwriting beyond memory

Laurus Edelbacher

2022

Overwriting Beyond Memory explores the nature of digital information. Through a processing script, it continuously captures and stores screenshots of current news images in a folder.

The stored images are intricately arranged in a mosaic pattern, gradually creating a new, dynamic image.

This process is intentionally slowed by the hard drive's technical limitations, mirroring the lag in our collective digital memory and media absorption.

As the artwork evolves, each image in the array undergoes a process of being overwritten.
